ORDER REQUIRING AFFIANT SIGNATURE

Upon review of the Affidavit of Probable Cause submitted in support of the Criminal Information in the above-captioned case, the Court finds that the affidavit contains an unsigned notation, "/s/ SIGNATURE," attributed to "DIVISION" of the Department of Justice.

Pursuant to Rule 3 of the Mayflower Rules of Criminal Procedure, affidavits supporting criminal complaints must be sworn to and signed by a person — not a division or entity — to properly establish probable cause.

Accordingly, it is hereby ORDERED that the Government shall ensure the Affidavit of Probable Cause is corrected to reflect a valid signature by a specific individual authorized to attest to the facts under oath.

The Government shall file the corrected affidavit with this Court no later than 48 hours from the issuance of this Order.

SO ORDERED.
