# Application for a Search Warrant by Alternative Means

As an alternative to a written affidavit, I allow warrant applications to be submitted by sworn testimony in accordance with accepted procedural standards (modeled on Rule 41(d)(3), Fed. R. Crim. P.). This process is intended for use when time, safety, or logistics make a written affidavit impractical. Rule 2(1)(3), Mayfl R. Crim P., allows me great discretion on how to accept a search warrant application, local rules aside.

Before contacting me for a warrant by these means, you must be prepared to provide:
1. Your full username, badge number (if applicable), and agency;
2. The exact location to be searched;
3. What items you are seeking;
4. Clear, specific facts supporting probable cause (e.g., observations, statements, informants, videos); and
5. Why the situation requires an oral application (e.g., urgency or no access to affidavit tools).

To request a warrant by these means, you must message me directly via DM asking me to process you. I will then open a private thread in this channel titled "In Re Search Warrant Application (MC-####-##)," which I will invite you to. You will then be placed under oath and required to testify truthfully and completely; you are expected to present a full record with detailed exhibits. The testimony will be recorded and logged as part of the official record.

Once the warrant is issued, you must file an inventory return after execution, and provide it in the same thread channel. I may require a brief summary of the oral testimony for the record (case-dependent).
