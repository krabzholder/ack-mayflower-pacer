1 M.S.C. 2307 Criminal threats


PLEA AGREEMENT
Both the prosecution and defense have agreed to a plea agreement. Within this agreement, the Defendant shall plead guilty to a single count of battery. In accordance with the plea agreement, the second charge of Aggravated Battery was dismissed. The Defendant agreed to instead pay a citation in relation to the dismissed first count. The plea agreement includes agreed on sentencing recommendations, which are as follows:

The Defendant shall pay a fine of $300 for the citation.
