# NOTICE: Summary Judgment granted in [bemyfrenemy1832 v. State Residency Office, et al.](https://courts.clarkcounty.site/view/460)

The District Court has granted summary judgment in favor of the Plaintiff, enjoining enforcement of the July 10, 2025 executive decree revoking Plaintiffs residency. All enforcement actions under the revoked order are permanently restrained unless and until procedural due process is afforded, in line with Article V, Section 3, Clause 4 of the Mayflower Constitution ("The Governor may individually grant or revoke residency, as regulated by the legislature; but such decisions shall be subject to review by a Court of Law.").
